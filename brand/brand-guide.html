06 · Brand Don'ts

What Not to Do

❌ No ship's wheels, anchors, or nautical imagery. The name "Helm" means control and direction. The metaphor works in the name. Making it literal in the logo puts you in Bold Care / Man Matters territory.
❌ No medical crosses, caduceus, or stethoscope icons. You're not a hospital. You're a premium treatment platform. The "Rx" in the wordmark does all the medical signaling you need.
❌ No glossy packaging, candy colors, or Instagram-gradient finishes. The brand architecture says it clearly: "HelmRx packaging should look like it belongs in a doctor's office, not on an Instagram influencer's shelf."
❌ No before/after photos on packaging. DCGI will flag this. No percentage claims on boxes. No comparison claims. No celebrity endorsements on physical product.
❌ No coupon codes, flash sale banners, or "limited time" urgency. The brand architecture explicitly prohibits these. Savings come through subscription tier commitment, not desperation marketing.
